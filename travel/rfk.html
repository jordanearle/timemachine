DATE: 05/06/1968 LOCATION: LOS ANGELES, CA

THE LOS ANGELES TRIBUNE 05/06/1968

Kennedy is Dead, Victim of Assassin; Suspect, Arab Immigrant, Arraigned;

Senator Robert F. Kennedy, the brother of a murdered President, died at 1:44 A.M. today of an assassin's shots. The New York Senator was wounded more than 20 hours earlier, moments after he had made his victory statement in the California primary

At his side when he died today in Good Samaritan Hospital were his wife Ethel; his sisters, Mrs. Stephen Smith and Mrs. Patricia Lawford; his bother-in-law, Stephen Smith; and his sister-in-law, Mrs. John F. Kennedy, whose husband was assassinated 4 1/2 years ago in Dallas.

In Washington, President Johnson issued a statement calling the death a tragedy. He proclaimed next Sunday a national day of mourning.
